Good Morning, Breakfast is Here

Fresh baked goods for breakfast whenever you want Brotchengold brings them to your front door early in the morning. Week after week. On one or more days, on weekdays and also on weekends. Save yourself the trip to the bakery and gain valuable time in the early morning.

Brotchengold breakfast services have been supplying breakfast friends punctually and reliably with baked goods from regional craft bakeries since 1991. More than 100,000 households in Germany and Austria now use this pleasant and environmentally conscious service.
